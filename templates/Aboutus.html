About Us

Online Face Recognition Attendance System (OFRAS) is a CSE Final Year Project made by Group No. 31 of GES R H Sapat College of Engineering.

About Our Mission

To automate and simplify the attendance system with online face recognition attendance system.

About Our Product

Traditionally, studentâ€™s attendances during lecture session are taken manually by using attendance sheet given by the faculty in class, which is a time consuming event.
The online face recognition attendance system helps the faculty in a great way.
In this project, the Open CV based face recognition approach has been proposed.
This model integrates a camera that captures an input image, an algorithm for detecting face and then marking the attendance.
